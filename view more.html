Pumpkin Soup

restaurant 4 servings
schedule 30 minutes
Soup Vegetarian Gluten-Free

Ingredients

  • 500 ml milk
  • 50 g butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp cocoa
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • A pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Heat the milk in a saucepan.
  2. Add the butter and stir until melted.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl.
  4. Slowly add the eggs to the milk, stirring continuously.
  5. Add the cocoa, baking soda, and salt.
  6. Simmer until thickened.

Notes

Serve with bread for a complete meal.
